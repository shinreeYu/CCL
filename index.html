[ THE ENDLESS TREE ]

Scientific Name:

Adansonia theEndlessTree

Discovered by / at:

TeamSY, 2106.04.26

Family:

'The Endless Tree' has long been widely recognized as in the family of Adansonia ever since its existence due to similar appearance. However, there have always been debates because of the very different habits of the Endless Tree and Adansonia. This question may soon be solved concerning that the new technology break may enable people to actually land in one of the galaxies that the Endless Tree may live on and very likely to collect the Endless Tree back (although of course we don't want them to grow here!)

Habitat:

Any random galaxy that they happen to come to (luckily, they haven't come to any place near the Earth)

Appearance:

The endless trees usually have brown seeds and its branches is slightly lighter than the seeds in color, with its leave in outstanding green (surprising it took us so long to find them). The branches typically grows for 1 month and then stop, with its leave growing forever (now we can control this a little bit with laser), eventually making the planet it grows on exhausted and exploded.

Key Characteristics:

  • Grow forever.
  • Stay wherever it flies to.
